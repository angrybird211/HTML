Achieving World Peace through Blockchain

We will continue to provide new experiences and value to all users of Blockchain XX.

The current blockchain faces a number of problems.
The value of cryptographic assets will disappear overnight due to quantum computers.
It will have a negative impact on the global environment because it will increase carbon dioxide emissions.
The storage capacity will reach its limit and the service will become unusable.

XX is a new blockchain that goes beyond the limits of conventional blockchains.
To overcome the worst situation described above, we have succeeded in discovering and developing a new blockchain with high security based on Proof of Authority (PoA). It is a breakthrough that can solve all the problems of conventional blockchains. Our vision is to use this new blockchain XX to achieve world peace.

XX chain is a security-focused chain.

Quantum computer-resistant cryptography
Capable of using cryptography to communicate in secret
Prevents all kinds of hacking

Can fundamentally prevent hacking of the blockchain

We need a centralized blockchain.

The blockchain so far has been a decentralized system. With this system, there is a possibility that we will not be able to control it at all in the near future. In order to solve this problem and make the blockchain trustworthy, we are using the centralized PoA protocol.

We have a clear roadmap.

A currency that constantly fluctuates no longer makes sense. You cannot put your trust in something that fluctuates, so it cannot be used by all people. A stable price will make the currency usable by all, so we will provide a stable currency with our own algorithm.

Setting the stage for a financial revolution

For too long, financial institutions have neglected innovation at the expense of their customers, and XX promises to go beyond being a currency to become an open platform for revolutionary finance and grow the real GDP of the blockchain economy.
